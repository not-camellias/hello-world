Camille's Selections from the CWD Index

  1. I Know Wheere Your Cat Lives (2014) by Owen Mundy.
    What I like about Mundy's feline site is how it presents a commentary on how easy personl data can be shared and tracked, in the cutest way possible.
  2. Data Will Help Us (2013) by Jonathan Harris.
    What I like about Harris' manifesto is how he juxtaposes the use of data with what it cannot replace.
  3. On a Mountain Top (2010) by Alex Fuller.
    What I like about Fuller's site is how collaborative it is despite the simple interface.
